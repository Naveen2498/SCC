Mission

Southeastern Caribbean College exists to develop visionary leaders and equip helping professionals with critical competencies, spiritual fervor, integrity, and an interdisciplinary approach to academic pursuit and life grounded in a Christian worldview through transformational education and research. 
Vision
Our vision at Southeastern Caribbean College is to become a world class educational institution by providing our students with a student-oriented learning community, the best of local and international instructors, educational mentorship and support, and high standards of scholarship and research.

Welcome Message

We live in a world that has become increasingly competitive, challenging, and even confusing to navigate. Developing a global perspective that is grounded in a robust worldview along with character qualities and critical competencies will help one successfully live and thrive in the current environment.

At the Southeastern Caribbean College, we provide our students with the level of exposure needed to achieve this goal and to be equipped to make a lasting impact on their families, workplace environment, and communities.
